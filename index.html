Fixed Costs & Depreciation

Investment costs that don't change with production level

Formula: Depreciation/year = Investment Cost ÷ Asset Lifespan (years)
Formula: Depreciation/cycle = Depreciation/year ÷ Number of cycles per year
Fixed costs are not affected by increases or decreases in production.
📦

Variable Costs per Cycle

Costs that change with production volume

Variable costs increase or decrease with production. They include animals, feed, drugs, labor, and all operational expenses.
Animal purchase cost is auto-calculated from number of animals × price per animal.

Economic Analysis — Profitability Measures

Three credit scenarios compared with full formulas

Capital & Credit Formulas
Total Capital = Investment Costs + Variable Costs (excl. labor)
50% Credit Cost/year = Total Capital × 0.5 × Interest Rate
100% Credit Cost/year = Total Capital × 1.0 × Interest Rate
Total Costs (credit) = Total Costs (no credit) + Credit Cost
—
Profitability Indicators Formulas
% NP / TC = Net Profit ÷ Total Costs × 100
% TR / TC (B:C ratio) = Total Returns ÷ Total Costs × 100
Capital Return Rate = Net Profit ÷ Investment Costs × 100
Capital Cycle (years) = Investment Costs ÷ Net Profit
