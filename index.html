Fixed Costs & Depreciation

Investment costs that don't change with production level

Depreciation/year = Investment Cost ÷ Asset Lifespan (years)
Depreciation/cycle = Depreciation/year ÷ Number of cycles per year
Fixed costs are not affected by increases or decreases in production.
📦

Variable Costs per Cycle

Costs that change with production volume

Variable costs increase or decrease with production. They include chicks, feed, drugs, labor, and all operational expenses.
Chick cost is auto-calculated from the number of chicks × price per chick.

Total Returns

Birds sold per cycle = Chicks × (1 − Mortality%)
Broiler sales = Birds sold × Avg. weight (kg) × Price per kg
Additional revenue: litter (bedding) sales, and any other income.
